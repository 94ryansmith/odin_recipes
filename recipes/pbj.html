Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

How to make a pbj sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Fruit Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients listed above
  2. Lay out bread
  3. Open peanut butter and jelly jar
  4. Grab utensil to spread out peanut butter
  5. Spread peanut butter evenly
  6. Next spread out jelly
  7. Combine bread then enjoy!
