Buttered Toast

How to make butter toast

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients listed above
  2. You need a toaster and a knife
  3. Cut butter with knife
  4. Keep butter on knife
  5. Smear butter on bread
  6. Place bread into toaster
  7. Let toaster cook bread for 20 to 60 seconds
  8. Remove bread from toaster
